Privacy & Data Protection Policy
KOLOSVIT ASBL (Luxembourg)

1. General Provisions

This Policy is established by KOLOSVIT ASBL (RCS F15616), a non-profit organisation registered in Luxembourg. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). By using kolosvit.eu and participating in our surveys, you agree to this Policy.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process data solely for our statutory goals, including:

  • Analysing the needs of veterans and refugees for rehabilitation programmes.
  • Implementing the ARCH project (Assessment · Resilience · Cognitive Hub) and STEM initiatives.
  • Communication with members, volunteers, and partners.
  • Reporting to donors and grantors (in anonymised form).

3. Data We Collect

We only collect data provided voluntarily:

  • Identity: name, email, phone number.
  • Socio-demographic: status (veteran, refugee, student), interests, and support needs.
  • Technical: cookies for Google Analytics.

4. Anonymity and Security

Our surveys can be completed anonymously. For any personal data provided, we implement modern security measures to prevent loss or unauthorised access.

5. Data Sharing

KOLOSVIT ASBL does not sell your data. Data may be shared with:

  • Luxembourg authorities (as required by law).
  • Donors and auditors (exclusively as aggregated statistics).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data, and to withdraw your consent at any time.

7. Contact

RCS Luxembourg: F15616
