About KOLOSVIT

KOLOSVIT ASBL is an innovative hub in Luxembourg where future technologies serve people. Digital education, mental health and cultural integration for a resilient community.

Registered 25 March 2026 · ASBL · at least 90% of resources dedicated to the mission.

“We are united by shared values — humanity, knowledge, and the belief that every talent matters.”

🖥 Digital Education — Autonomous systems training. 🧠 Neuro-Resilience — ARCH programme. 🤝 Social Integration — Wellness & culture. 🌿 Environment — Ecological responsibility.

Join the Network

Membership in the network is based on mutual exchange. We ask two questions:

✱ What are you looking for?

✱ What are you ready to offer?

Those who only seek without contributing — even as a volunteer — do not join this network. This is not rigidity; it is the principle of a living community where everyone both gives and receives.

KOLOSVIT ASBL is a member of

ARCH Alliance

ARCH — Alliance for Resilience, Community & Humanitarian Action

An aggregator of existing European and international best practices in resilience and civil preparedness. A platform of community resilience at the intersection of civil protection, territorial defence, youth training, crisis response and dual-use technologies.

Network & Partners

KOLOSVIT ASBL represents a network of partners with whom memoranda of cooperation have been signed:

  • 48 Ukrainian enterprises
  • 5 Ukrainian civil society organisations
  • 1 Ukrainian charitable foundation
  • 3 cooperation agreements involving Ukrainian expertise

The network includes technology partners, including LuGus Studios (Belgium), united on the ARCH international platform. We invite other technology partners to collaborate. We also hold a MoU with Velosidrón, and are open to universities, educational centres and public institutions.

In May 2026 we launched our first pilot project promoting digital education and drone simulator training. The project currently operates 6 Liftoff stations and 1 training classroom.

What ARCH bridges
🏝 Civil Protection
Coordinating preparedness at community level with existing civil defence structures.
🛡 Territorial Resilience
Supporting civil preparedness without militarisation — using the language of Community Resilience and Societal Resilience now present in every second NATO and EU document.
🎓 Youth Training
Scouts, Plast, cadet programmes and youth organisations — practical skills without military framing.
🚨 Crisis Response
Volunteer teams, paramedics, search and rescue — structured and coordinated.
🤖 Dual-Use Technologies
Drone operators, mapping, forest fire monitoring, search operations — civilian application of advanced tools.
ARCH Modules
Six pillars of the platform
🏠 ARCH Civil
For the general population

First aid · Self-help · Emergency preparedness · Fire safety · Evacuation · Autonomous living.
🪗 ARCH Scout
Youth organisations

Scouts, Plast, youth organisations and cadet programmes. No militarisation — but real, practical skills.
🚑 ARCH Rescue
Emergency response

Paramedicine · Search and rescue · Crisis response · Volunteer teams.
🚁 ARCH Drone
Assessment, Resilience & Cognitive Hub for autonomous systems operators

UAV operators · Mapping · Forest fire monitoring · Search operations · Civil protection. Far easier to present to Europe than “military drones”.
🏠 ARCH Shelter
Infrastructure

Shelter inventory · Restoring abandoned facilities · Basement adaptation · Crisis centres.
🌳 ARCH Resilience Campus
Physical hub

Former camp, school, farm, sanatorium or campsite — repurposed for youth, volunteers, civil protection, reservists and humanitarian missions.
Most valuable for Benelux & Europe

Not “territorial defence” — a term that makes many uneasy. Instead:

Community Resilience · Civil Preparedness · Societal Resilience

These terms appear in literally every second NATO and EU document today. ARCH as aggregator — bringing together best practices, organisations and technologies under one coordinated platform — is the unique value proposition.
