About KOLOSVIT

KOLOSVIT ASBL is an innovative association registered in Luxembourg, where future technologies serve real human needs. We believe that digital education, mental resilience and cultural belonging are not luxuries — they are foundations for a dignified life in a new country.

Founded by Ukrainians living in Europe, KOLOSVIT builds bridges between communities, generations and disciplines. We work at the intersection of culture, science and social care — always led by humanity first.

At least 90% of our resources go directly to our mission: supporting displaced people, developing educational programmes and fostering meaningful cultural connections across Europe.

“We are united by shared values — humanity, knowledge, and the belief that every talent matters. Every person who arrives carrying loss also arrives carrying gifts. Our role is to help those gifts bloom.”

🖥 Digital Education — Autonomous systems, AI literacy and future-skills training.
🧠 Neuro-Resilience — The ARCH programme for cognitive health and stress recovery.
🤝 Social Integration — Cultural events, wellness and community belonging.
🌿 Environment — Ecological responsibility as a civic value.

Les Perles d'Onyx · Pearls of Luxembourg

Stories · Skills · Connections

Pearls of Luxembourg is an initiative of KOLOSVIT ASBL and its CEO Jan Onyx (pen name) that brings together remarkable people through inspiring stories, practical knowledge and meaningful human connections.

Luxembourg is full of people whose stories deserve to be heard beyond their own circle. Pearls creates the space where these stories are shared — and where new connections begin.

Pearls is not just an evening series. It is a growing ecosystem — adaptable to any sector, any community, any story worth telling.

Our Formats
💎 Pearls Talks
Three guests. Three stories. Three dreams for Luxembourg. An intimate evening where entrepreneurs, scientists, artists and professionals share their experience. Each guest shares a story from their professional world, something about Luxembourg they wish more people knew, and a dream — what they would change, if they could.
💎 Pearls Workshops
Learning directly from remarkable people. Every guest answers one simple question: "What can I teach people in one hour?"

Topics include drone flying, photography, leadership, AI, logistics, public speaking, cooking, dance, wellbeing, entrepreneurship, first aid, creativity — and many others.
💎 Pearls Together
A programme for people open to building a lifelong partnership and family. Not speed dating. Not a dating app.

At registration, guests may indicate their openness to meaningful connection. Those who do receive a small heart on their badge — visible, but never intrusive. Every participant joins on the basis of shared values and serious intentions.
💎 Pearls Podcast
Conversations that continue beyond the event. Interviews with inspiring guests, innovators, entrepreneurs and community builders — available to audiences far beyond Luxembourg.
💎 Pearls Charity
Selected evenings include charity auctions or initiatives supporting cultural, educational or humanitarian causes. Every pearl can create new opportunities for others.
💎 Pearls Media
Every story deserves to be preserved. Professional photography and short documentaries capture each evening — creating content for media partners, employer branding and wider distribution.
The Relay Model

Every Pearls evening begins with people and ends with new connections. After each evening, every guest recommends the next remarkable person — and possibly the next location. The network grows organically, one story at a time.

"I am not looking for celebrities. I am looking for people who connect people."

What to Expect
  • 3 guests · 3 stories · one evening
  • Live music · informal networking
  • Tea and coffee included
  • Professional photography and video (with consent)
Badge System
  • 🟡 Entry — included with every ticket
  • ❤️ Pearls Together — open to meaningful connection
  • 🍴 Culinary option — at selected evenings

Alcohol is not part of the Pearls experience. Food, when present, is always part of the programme — never a standard buffet.

Partners & Locations

Pearls evenings take place in remarkable spaces across Luxembourg — cultural centres, private galleries, historic spaces, wellness destinations such as Mondorf-les-Bains, and partner restaurants or studios.

We welcome long-term venue partnerships. Providing a space for Pearls evenings is a meaningful in-kind contribution — and a natural opportunity to connect with an engaged, values-driven community.

Partnership Opportunities
  • 🤝 Venue Partner — Host a Pearls evening in your space
  • 🍽️ Culinary Partner — Present your cuisine at a selected evening
  • 📸 Media Partner — Co-produce content and documentary material
  • 🎓 Workshop Partner — Bring your expertise as a guest instructor
  • 🌍 Institutional Partner — Support Pearls through sponsorship or grants
Business Model

Pearls of Luxembourg is an initiative of KOLOSVIT ASBL — a non-profit association. Our model is transparent and built for long-term sustainability.

🎟️ Individual Ticket
Entry to every Pearls evening is ticketed. Tea and coffee are included. Each evening has a recommended contribution reflecting the format and programme. Additional donations are always welcome.
🏢 Corporate Participation
Companies may reserve places for their teams — priority registration and optional recognition as an evening partner.
🤝 Partnership in Kind
Venues, culinary partners and media partners contribute through their space, product or expertise — recognised as a valued contribution to the Pearls ecosystem.
🌍 Grants & Sponsorship
Organisations and foundations may sponsor selected evenings or the programme as a whole. Once documented, Pearls will be eligible for FNR, EDF and sector innovation funding.

Our Vision

Pearls of Luxembourg is more than a series of evenings. It is a long-term community where people inspire one another, learn from experts, create new projects, build friendships — and sometimes even create families.

Our goal is to build one of Europe's most trusted communities — rooted in Luxembourg, open to the world.

Because every pearl begins with something small — and grows into something lasting.

ARCH Alliance

Aerospace Resilience & Cognitive Health

⚙️ In Development

ARCH Resilience Hub is a structured prevention programme that combines AI-assisted wellbeing monitoring, individual consultations and resilience activities into one integrated approach — tailored to the specific operational and psychosocial challenges of each sector.

ARCH does not treat. ARCH detects, informs and recommends. Each participant receives a personalised recovery plan developed with a consultant — not a medical diagnosis, but an actionable wellbeing pathway.

Our Pilot Programmes

Pilot Programme · Financial Sector

ARCH Resilience Hub for Financial Institutions

The Problem: Compliance, AML, Risk and Operations teams work under constant cognitive overload — regulatory deadlines, personal accountability and high-pressure multilingual environments increase the risk of burnout, loss of focus and staff turnover. Luxembourg has one of the highest burnout rates in the EU financial workforce.

What We Offer

  • AI-assisted wellbeing monitoring
  • Early burnout detection
  • Individual resilience consultations
  • Resilience Hub sessions
  • Cognitive recovery activities
  • Anonymous management reporting
  • ESG wellbeing metrics
  • Voluntary employee participation

For Whom

  • Banks
  • Investment Funds
  • AML Teams
  • Compliance Departments
  • FinTech
  • Insurance
  • Financial Service Providers

Key Benefits

  • Earlier burnout detection
  • Higher employee resilience
  • Lower absenteeism
  • Better staff retention
  • Support for ESG initiatives
  • Privacy-first approach
  • EU AI Act aware design

Pilot budgets start from approximately €10,000 per month. A detailed proposal is prepared after an introductory discussion. INFPC co-funding (~15%) available via Luxembourg State.

Pilot Programme · Logistics & Transport

ARCH Resilience Hub for Transport Operators

The Problem: Drivers, dispatchers, ground coordinators and aviation operators face chronic fatigue, shift work and high-stakes decisions. EU NIS2 Directive classifies rail and road freight as critical infrastructure — human resilience is now a compliance issue. Fatigue-related incidents remain the leading human factor in European transport safety.

What We Offer

  • Physiological fatigue monitoring
  • Pre-shift readiness screening
  • Simulator-based state assessment
  • Recovery Hub between shifts
  • Peer support groups
  • Anonymous safety reporting
  • NIS2 compliance support

For Whom

  • Rail operators (CFL)
  • Road freight companies
  • Cargo & aviation (Cargolux, Luxair)
  • Postal & logistics operators
  • Dispatchers & coordinators

Key Benefits

  • Reduced fatigue-related incidents
  • Measurable safety improvement
  • NIS2 compliance evidence
  • Lower sick leave
  • Better crew retention
  • ESG safety metrics

Pilot budgets from approximately €10,000–12,000 per month (basic version) / 15–20 operators. Final proposal after initial discussion.

Pilot Programme · Education & Integration

Simulator-Based Training for Youth and Refugees

Using Liftoff drone flight simulators (6 licences, MoU signed), ARCH develops practical training programmes for young people and refugees — building real technical skills, focus and resilience through simulation. We are currently determining the location and equipment for our first training classroom.

Programme Elements

  • Drone flight simulation training
  • Focus and decision-making exercises
  • Stress resilience development
  • Certification pathways
  • Community integration

Target Participants

  • Young people (16–25)
  • Refugees and displaced persons
  • NEET youth (not in education or employment)
  • Integration programme participants
