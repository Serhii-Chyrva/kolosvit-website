Grand Théâtre · Philharmonie · MUDAM
Together with the Red Cross Luxembourg, we organised 8 cultural excursions — approximately 10 participants per event — to the Grand Théâtre, Philharmonie Luxembourg, and the MUDAM contemporary art museum. These outings gave Ukrainian refugees a chance to connect with Luxembourg’s cultural life, find moments of joy and beauty, and feel part of the European community.

Culture Helps Solidarity
In partnership with NGO “Strings of Heart”, we submitted a joint application for psychological and cultural support for Ukrainian refugees across Luxembourg, the Netherlands and Finland. The programme combines arts therapy, community events and mental resilience workshops — addressing trauma, isolation and the challenge of rebuilding life in a new country.
Under review
LSA — ARCH Platform
The ARCH (Aerospace Resilience & Cognitive Health) platform is our most ambitious project: a simulation environment combining aerospace training methodologies with cognitive load awareness tools. Designed for high-stress professional settings, ARCH also has direct application in refugee support and mental resilience training. The first public phase is currently under preparation.

About KOLOSVIT

KOLOSVIT ASBL is an innovative association registered in Luxembourg, where future technologies serve real human needs. We believe that digital education, mental resilience and cultural belonging are not luxuries — they are foundations for a dignified life in a new country.

Founded by Ukrainians living in Europe, KOLOSVIT builds bridges between communities, generations and disciplines. We work at the intersection of culture, science and social care — always led by humanity first.

At least 90% of our resources go directly to our mission: supporting displaced people, developing educational programmes and fostering meaningful cultural connections across Europe.

“We are united by shared values — humanity, knowledge, and the belief that every talent matters. Every person who arrives carrying loss also arrives carrying gifts. Our role is to help those gifts bloom.”

🖥 Digital Education — Autonomous systems, AI literacy and future-skills training.
🧠 Neuro-Resilience — The ARCH programme for cognitive health and stress recovery.
🤝 Social Integration — Cultural events, wellness and community belonging.
🌿 Environment — Ecological responsibility as a civic value.
