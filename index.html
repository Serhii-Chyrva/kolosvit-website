Who We Are

About Kolosvit
& Contact

KOLOSVIT ASBL is an innovative hub in Luxembourg where future technologies serve people. We combine digital education, mental health and cultural integration to build a resilient community.

Registered in Luxembourg on 25 March 2026 as an ASBL with strictly non-commercial, public-interest goals — at least 90% of resources dedicated to the mission. We apply high standards of transparency and good governance.

"We are united by shared values — humanity, knowledge, and the belief that every talent matters."

🖥️ Digital Education (Future Skills) — Training in autonomous systems management using professional simulators. We prepare specialists for civil protection, logistics and environmental monitoring. 🧠 Mental Resilience (Neuro-Resilience) — The unique ARCH programme: combining robotics and neuro-psychology for stress management and cognitive enhancement. 🤝 Social Integration (Community & Culture) — Uniting the Ukrainian community through wellness events, yoga and cultural evenings, helping refugees find new careers and integrate into Luxembourg society. 🌿 Environment & Animals — Ecological responsibility and animal welfare.
